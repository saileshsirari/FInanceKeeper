Automatic Expense Tracking

Finance Keeper automatically detects expenses from bank transaction SMS and converts them into categorized spending records.

  • Detect transaction amount
  • Identify merchant automatically
  • Group expenses into categories

Powerful Expense Management

  • Bulk edit similar transactions in one go
  • Move multiple merchants into new categories
  • Merge or rename merchants
  • Re-categorize many expenses quickly

Because bank SMS formats vary across banks, these tools make it easy to correct or organize expenses in seconds.

Privacy First

Finance Keeper is designed to keep your financial data private.

  • No bank login required
  • No financial data uploaded
  • All SMS processing happens locally
  • Your data stays on your device
